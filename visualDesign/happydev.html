DESIGN RATIONALE

The rationale behind developing the “Happy Haptics” motion graphic is to take advantage of visual communication on its strong capability of invoking emotions and memories. Studies found that visual art can bring therapeutic benefits for improving self-esteem, self-confidence (Deirdre Heenan 184-188). Since anxiety, stress, as well as happiness and joyfulness, is usually perceived than to be verbalized, visual communication is a more expressive method to be applied to therapies, as said that Creative Arts Therapies (CATs) or arts interventions are regarded as an innovative way to prevent stress and improve stress management (Martin). Besides, visual graphics is also a medium for raising public awareness about social issues, as many public posters, motion graphics, videos have already shown, by communicating with the public via media, it potentially brings up social attention to social issues. Henceforward, with lively rhythms and music to create “Happy Haptics” motion graphics, it is to demonstrate the idea of “happiness as a tangible remedy to social digital wellbeing.”

Deirdre Heenan, “Art as therapy: an effective way of promoting positive mental health?” Disability & Society, 21:2, 179-191, 2006, DOI: 10.1080/09687590500498143 Martin, Lily et al. “Creative Arts Interventions for Stress Management and Prevention-A Systematic Review.” Behavioral sciences (Basel, Switzerland) vol. 8,2 28. 22 Feb. 2018, doi:10.3390/ bs8020028

DEVELOPMENT

The Gummy bears, and the joyful image it deliveries to people is something adorable and charming, therefore, it likes the key to let go our stress and reveal happiness or say a childish mind that pleases us which embed deeply in our heart. It's our stress, anxiety let the this very joy stayed hidden. A healing zone effects is what the motion graphic project trying to present to the audiences. With the ‘esp’ key from our keyboard, press the key so we can all escape.

The development of haptics went a long way of exploration, I was wondering how could visual illustrations give people the sense of “ah!” or “oh!” these sort of know without knowing impressions. Like some of these elements here, the radish charger, or the swan umbrella handle, it is to intrigue people’s memory about something tangible with a different texture, imagine a phone charger with the skin of a radish, or picture the feather slowing growing on your umbrella.

My explorations were helpful, nonetheless, it wasn’t quite there, in a way I couldn’t hear the resonance between the haptics and those visual creations. The ideation continued to discover life elements, then there comes the pleasantly chewy, intriguing, colorful, gummy bears and the bouncy, everyday-needed keyboard, both of them produces the similar tangibility, however, when it is visualized, it creates a contrast among these two life elements. The gummy bears are happy, you could hear enjoyable chewing sound, you may also hear children laugh by seeing the gummy bears. The keyboard, reminds you your work life, 9 - 7 routines in an office, the keyboard produces the sound of so called modernity. Together, I think I finally found the perfect subject for the journey of searching what’s happiness.

The end.
