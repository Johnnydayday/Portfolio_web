Members of 2019 G.C.T

The Global Career Tour (GCT) is an annual activity of Underwood International College of Yonsei University, every year, students and professors would visit world wide companies and corporations to study and communicate. In the year of 2019, the GCT team was delegated to visit Tokyo, Japan; In the tour there were nine organizations or institutes that the students and professors visited, including Hitachi, Tiktok(Japan), TOTO, the University of Tokyo, etc.
