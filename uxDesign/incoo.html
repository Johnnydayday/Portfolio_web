Incoo: Get Useful Phrases by Situation

"Are Users The Only Ones That I am Designing For?"
Time: 2019,10

Solo traveling sounds exciting. But one moment had definitely frustrated solo travellers trips was when landed in a foreign place. And especially, when one was lost, when one was hungry, even when one was injured during adventures. It’s so easy to “solve” all these by searching on Google Map about where to go; by asking Siri about where to eat, she can also do translation now, etc. What’s next? Assuming now, a solo traveller are going to a restaurant. “你好，想吃点什么？ (What would you like to eat?)” He/she would be like: “oh, wait a minute, let me use a translator…” Then the fluidity of conversation breaks down. It is an issue for solo traveler community members. Because of the language barrier, the hardship of dealing with instant conversation with the local people become a significant struggle.


Designs


DESIGN

Incoo, a mobile application that helps with improving communication efficiency for solo travelers. Incoo stands for “Instant Conversation,” as the app’s primary goal is to improve communication efficiency between solo travelers and the local people. Incoo takes advantage of mobile GPS, map service, and online translator. The Geo-location and map service can provide information of where is the user and what’s around the user; when users surrounding information is processed, then the map will display different pin annotations regarding nearby stores, restaurants, malls, beauty salons, and local pharmacies. Every annotation is clickable so that users can click in and see provided phrases. (Contents are displayed in both languages - the users’ mother tongue and the local language).
