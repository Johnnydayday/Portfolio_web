Book Design

Brave New World

Time: 2019,10

The Storybook is the expansion of the Storyboard project, which is about to build the contents and visualize in 20 Pages book format. It is required the study of the grid system and typography usage within a length. There should be images and text to structure the book in an appropriate amount of usage.

Photos: Ansel Adams, Aaron Siskind, Brassai, Berenice Abbott, Helmut Newton, Henri Catier Bresson, Paul Strand, Walker Evans.



DESIGN

The first phase for me to design the storybook was to find the matching contents. What story shall the book have? I found the book: “Brave New World” the most interesting to me. Although I haven’t read the entire book, I was very attracted by the contents of the book while I was reading some paragraphs. It is indeed a thought-provoking novel. After deciding the contents, I went through the process of selecting images. I choose to use the same photography work by artists that were given by previous projects. As I go through again the artwork by these photographers, I found the connection between many photos and the story itself. Therefore, I wanted to design a storybook that encourages thoughtful reading as well as to deliver the themes of power, humanity, struggle, and hope from “Brave New World.” To keep it the book simple, I adopted a two collum grid, and 40mm margins on each side. To discover the suitable typeface for my design, I explored various typefaces and finally decided on using Eloquent JF Pro, Palatino, and Seravek.
