Motion Graphic

Happy Haptics

Time: 2020.12

Let the Gummy Bear Bring You Happiness" Happy Haptics is a motion graphic about the work-life and happiness. The video narrates a story of the Gummy Bears appear in a person's work life. While the keys on the keyboard are something tangible, bouncy, it is the representation of endless office hours and tedious productivity. On the other hand, the gummy bears are pleasantly chewing, although slightly glutinous, the sweet taste can always remind us of joyfulness.
